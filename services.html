NEFT & RTGS

NEFT & RTGS

भारतातील कोणत्याही बँकेत मोठ्या रकमेचे सुरक्षित आणि जलद निधी हस्तांतरण (Fund Transfer) करण्यासाठी योग्य सेवा.

Fast and highly secure nationwide money transfer service to any bank across India using NEFT and RTGS systems.

ATM & RuPay Cards

Get our co-branded RuPay ATM Debit Card and withdraw cash from any bank ATM machine anywhere in India, anytime.

UPI QR Payments

Make instant payments directly from your bank account to any mobile number or merchant QR code using BHIM UPI.
